Airport Hacks & Travel Tips

Small changes that make a big difference

🅿️

Book parking 3 months ahead

Lock in the best rates and save up to 50% compared to last-minute booking. Off-site parking can be half the price of on-site.

⏰

Arrive 2-3 hours early

Domestic flights: 2 hours. International: 3 hours. Early morning flights have fewer delays and less stress.

🎒

Pack snacks for kids

Airport food is expensive. Bring protein bars, nuts, or sandwiches. Use packing cubes to stay organized.

🎮

Save "travel toys" for flights

Novelty holds attention longer. Keep special toys and activities just for travel days.

🏃

Find the play area

Most airports have dedicated play zones. Let kids burn off energy before boarding—check your airport's website.

🚗

Meet & greet = terminal drop-off

Drive straight to the terminal. A chauffeur meets you, parks your car, and brings it back when you land. No shuttles, no stress.
